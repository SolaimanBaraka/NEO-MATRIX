Our History

In 1949, Francesc Solà founded our store with the vision of offering quality products to the Figueres community. For decades, the store became a local reference, known for its excellent service and first-class products.

In 1975, Francesc Oliver took over the business, modernizing the store and expanding its product catalog. Under his direction, the store reached its peak, becoming a meeting point for Figueres residents.

However, in 2010, the economic crisis forced the store to close, leaving a void in the heart of Figueres.

In 2025, Solaiman Baraka Aoudi and Iker Damian Lluch Bellizzi, two entrepreneurs passionate about tradition and innovation, decided to revive this iconic establishment. With a modern vision while respecting the store's rich history, they have created a space that combines the best of the past with current trends.
